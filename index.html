Three abandoned children. Two opposing elements. One evil queen.
The power of flames against frost.
Princess Topaz was the heir to the throne of Crystalline. But now?
She's nothing. Topaz is an outcast, living with her siblings, Alexander and Leila, in the wilderness surrounding the city. Topaz’s wicked mother, the queen of Crystalline, is a cruel and oppressive tyrant, willing to execute anyone who stands in her way.
Join Topaz as she fights for her right to the throne– and for thefreedom of her future citizens. Will she succeed in restoring peace throughout the kingdom? Or will the queen destroy Topaz, her siblings, and all of Crystalline?
